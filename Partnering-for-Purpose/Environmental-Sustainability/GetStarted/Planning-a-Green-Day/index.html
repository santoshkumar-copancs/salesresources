Why it matters?

According to the United Nations and the World Health Organization climate change is the single biggest health threat to humanity, with 3.6 billion people already living in areas highly susceptible to the impacts of climate change.

A critical component in tackling climate change lies in businesses embracing technology.

As businesses, leading the way in manufacturing and implementing information and communications technology, we can partner together to help play our part in tackling this crucial issue.

What you can do?
  • Organise cross-company information sessions with subject matter experts
  • Create a green list of local NGOs where you can volunteer and use your tech expertise
  • Plan a cross-team Green Day and explore ways to build next-generation sustainable solutions for your customers

Possible charitable organizations

  • Earthjustice
  • Natural Resources Defense Council
  • World Wildlife Fund
