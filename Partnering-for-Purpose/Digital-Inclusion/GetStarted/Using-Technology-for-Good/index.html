Why it matters?

Charitable organizations often struggle with understanding what is needed and how to deploy, fund, and acquire the basic networking and collaboration infrastructure required to support their efforts to address the needs of their beneficiaries.

Reliable and suitable equipment can sometimes be the difference between life and death.

What you can do?

Lend your industry skills and experience to make an impact for a cause important to you. Get started by contacting Cisco's Product Grant Program team to help you understand the needs of the organization you want to support, determine what resources or materials are available, and help with the grant application.
