Why it matters?

Digital skills are essential for global competitiveness, providing job opportunities, and growth for communities. Women are 20% less likely to be online and the percentage rises in lower-income countries. Building digital skills at an early age, providing technical skills training, and engaging with role models is central to driving digital equality and ensuring more diverse people have the skills necessary to participate and prosper in our digital world.

What you can do?

Team up with your local community to deliver digital training or career sharing session to young generation. Leverage the platform and content of Women Rock-IT to provide technical skills training in your local community. Share your story to increase the impact.

Additional Ideas
  • Recruit volunteers to share their career journey and how they got into STEM.
  • Host a girls' night out watch party or host an event at your children’s school or community center.
