Why it matters?

Research shows that if you assemble a workforce of people who look, act, think, and behave differently, you are more likely to have a more successful and profitable organization McKinsey reported that addressing the diversity opportunity in technical talent should be an integral part of a company’s talent strategy.

It is therefore essential that companies have tools to engage and attract a diverse candidate pool.

What you can do?
  • Actively engage candidates from underrepresented communities through inclusive hiring
  • Establish partnerships with organizations that focus on diverse talent
  • Leverage Cisco’s Talent Bridge Matching Engine platform to help recruit and hire a more inclusive workforce.
