Why it matters?

Workplace volunteering events do more than benefit the beneficiaries, they foster meaningful relationships between employees and the company, and drive business outcomes. According to a study by National Council for Voluntary Organisation giveback activities enhance overall mental well-being, with over 77% of volunteers reporting improved mental health. Business benefits include an increase in customer loyalty and business growth (LA Business Journal, 2023).

What you can do?
  • Contact local non-profits and charities you can partner with and organize a team give-back
  • Incorporate a virtual giveback activity to your sales kick off or business event with FreeRice
  • Engage a social impact company like Give to Get to offload all the administration, operations and execution of your give back event
